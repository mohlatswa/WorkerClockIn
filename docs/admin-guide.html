WorkClock-In Administration Guide

Everything you need to manage workers, monitor attendance, and keep the system running smoothly.

What is WorkClock-In?

WorkClock-In is a staff attendance system that lets your workers clock in and out using their smartphone — from anywhere with internet access, as long as they are physically at the registered workplace location.

When a worker clocks in, the app records their GPS coordinates, the time, and how they authenticated (PIN, fingerprint, or face recognition). You can view all attendance records in the admin panel, filter by date or worker, and export to CSV for payroll.

Workers access the app through a company-specific link you share with them. The admin panel is accessible from the same app by scrolling to the bottom of the home screen and tapping Admin Login.

Admin Roles Explained

WorkClock-In has three admin roles. Your role determines what you can see and do.

Developer
Full system access
  • Manage all companies
  • Manage all admin accounts
  • Access all workers across companies
  • System configuration
Super Admin
Your company only
  • Manage your company's workers
  • View all attendance records
  • Create and manage Admin accounts
  • Configure workplaces
Admin
Day-to-day operations
  • Manage workers (add, edit, reset)
  • View attendance records
  • Export attendance CSV
  • Cannot create Admin accounts
ℹ

The Admins tab is only visible to Super Admin accounts. Regular Admin accounts will not see this tab.

Logging In to the Admin Panel

Open the worker portal link in your browser: https://mohlatswa.github.io/WorkerClockIn/

Scroll down on the home screen until you see the Admin Login button, or look for a small "Admin" link at the bottom. Tap it.

Enter your username and password, then tap Login.

You will be taken to the admin panel. If you are a Super Admin, you will see four tabs: Dashboard, Workers, Attendance, Admins, and Setup. Regular Admins see all except Admins.

🔒

Account lockout: After 5 incorrect password attempts, your account is locked for 30 minutes. Wait 30 minutes and try again, or ask a super admin or developer to unlock your account.

📧

Forgot your password? Tap the Forgot password? link below the Login button. Enter your username and tap Send OTP — a one-time code will be emailed to the address on your account. Enter the code, then set a new password. The OTP expires after 15 minutes. (You must have an email address saved on your account for this to work.)

ℹ

To log out, scroll to the bottom of any admin tab and tap Logout. You are returned to the home screen.

Dashboard Tab

Today at a Glance

The Dashboard shows four stat tiles for today's date:

  • Present: Number of workers who have clocked in at least once today.
  • Total Workers: Total active workers registered to your company.
  • Absent: Active workers who have not yet clocked in today. Tap this tile to jump straight to the Absent Today tab and see exactly who is missing.
  • Still In: Workers with an active clock-in (not yet clocked out).

Below the stats tiles is the Today's Activity Feed — a live list of today's clock-in and clock-out events, showing the worker's name, time, and whether they are still in or have clocked out. The most recent events appear at the top.

The dashboard reloads automatically each time you click the Dashboard tab. To see the latest data, click the tab again.

Workers Tab

This is where you manage your company's workforce. You can see all workers, their status, and what authentication methods they have enrolled.

Reading the Worker List

Each worker row shows their name, Employee ID, and a set of status indicators:

  • 🔒 — Account is locked (too many wrong PINs)
  • 📏 — Biometric (fingerprint/Face ID) is enrolled
  • 🤳 — Face is enrolled for face recognition
  • 📱 — Device is bound (they have signed in from a registered device)
  • Inactive — Worker account is disabled

Action Buttons

Each worker row has five action buttons on the right:

✏ Edit — Edit the worker's details (name, Employee ID, job title, PIN, workplace, phone, email).
🤳 Face — Open the face enrolment camera to enrol or re-enrol this worker's face.
📏 Biometric — Register a fingerprint or Face ID credential for this worker. The worker must be physically present on the device you are using.
🏝 Reset Security — Clear device binding, active session, and any account lockout. Use when a worker changes phone or is locked out.
🚫 / ✅ — Deactivate or reactivate the worker account.

Adding a Worker (Step by Step)

Tap the + Add Worker button at the top of the Workers tab to expand the add-worker panel.

Step 1 — Basic Details

Enter the Employee ID — a unique identifier for this worker (e.g. EMP001). This is what they type on the sign-in screen. Must be unique across all workers.

Enter the worker's Full Name as it should appear on records and greetings.

Optionally enter their Job Title (shown in the worker portal and attendance records).

Enter a PIN — minimum 4 digits, maximum 10 digits. Give this to the worker privately. They will use it to clock in.

Tap Add Worker. If successful, the form advances to Step 2 for optional enrolment.

Step 2 — Optional Enrolment

After the basic record is created, you can immediately enrol authentication methods:

  • Enrol Face: Opens the camera. Position the worker's face clearly in the frame and tap Capture. A single clear photo is all that is needed.
  • Register Biometric: Triggers a fingerprint/Face ID registration prompt. The worker must be present on this device and must complete the biometric prompt.

Both steps are optional. Tap Done to finish. The worker will appear in the list immediately.

ℹ

You can always come back and enrol biometrics later using the 🤳 and 📏 buttons on the worker row. The worker can also self-enrol their face and biometric from their own worker dashboard after signing in.

Editing a Worker

Tap the ✏ Edit button on the worker's row. A modal will open with their current details. You can update:

  • Full Name
  • Employee ID (must remain unique)
  • Job Title
  • PIN (leave blank to keep the existing PIN)
  • Assigned Workplace
  • Phone number
  • Email address

Tap Save to apply changes. The worker list will refresh automatically.

Deactivating a Worker

Tap the 🚫 button to deactivate a worker. A deactivated worker cannot sign in — the system will return "Employee ID not found" even if the ID is correct. Their attendance history is preserved.

Tap ✅ to reactivate them. This is immediate.

⚠

Deactivating a worker does not automatically clock them out if they are currently clocked in. Check the Dashboard for any active sessions before deactivating.

Enrolment Methods in Detail

Face Enrolment

Face enrolment captures a single still frame from the camera and extracts a 128-point facial embedding. Tips for best results:

  • Ensure the worker is in good lighting — avoid backlighting or harsh shadows.
  • The worker should face the camera directly, with a neutral expression.
  • The face should fill most of the oval guide on screen.
  • Glasses are acceptable but may reduce recognition accuracy slightly.
  • Re-enrol if the worker's appearance changes significantly (new glasses, beard, etc.).

Biometric Enrolment

Biometric enrolment uses the device's built-in authenticator (fingerprint scanner, Face ID, or Windows Hello). This process must be done on the same type of device and browser the worker will use to clock in, because the credential is tied to the platform.

  • The worker must be physically present.
  • The device must have biometrics enabled in its settings.
  • iOS Safari, Android Chrome, and desktop Chrome/Edge all support this.
  • If the worker changes their device, they need to be re-enrolled and their security reset first.

Re-Enrolment

To re-enrol either method, simply use the 🤳 (face) or 📏 (biometric) button again. The new data will overwrite the old data.

Attendance Tab

The Attendance tab is your reporting centre. Use the filters to find exactly the records you need.

Filters

  • From / To: Date range for the records. Defaults to the past month when you open the tab.
  • Worker: Filter to a specific worker. Defaults to "All Workers".
  • Method: Filter by authentication method — All, PIN, Biometric, or Face.

Tap Search to apply filters. The summary bar at the top of results shows total records, total hours, and how many workers are still clocked in.

Reading Attendance Records

Each record shows:

  • Worker name, Employee ID, and Job Title
  • Date of the record
  • Clock In time (green chip)
  • Clock Out time — shows "Still in" if the worker has not yet clocked out (red chip)
  • Hours worked (calculated difference)
  • Auth method used (PIN / Biometric / Face)

Exporting to CSV

With filters applied, tap Download CSV to export all matching records. The CSV file is named attendance_YYYY-MM-DD_to_YYYY-MM-DD.csv and includes: Worker Name, Employee ID, Job Title, Date, Clock In, Clock Out, Hours, Auth Method, and Status. It opens correctly in Microsoft Excel and Google Sheets.

Force Clock Out

On any attendance record where a worker is Still In (clock-out time is blank), a red Force Out button appears next to the record. Tap it to immediately set the clock-out time to now. Use this when a worker forgot to clock out at the end of their shift.

⚠

Force Out sets the clock-out time to the moment you tap the button — not the actual time the worker left. If you need accurate hours, note the correct time before using Force Out and adjust manually for payroll.

Absent Today Tab

The Absent tab shows a list of all active workers who have not clocked in today. This gives you a quick view of who is missing without needing to search through the attendance report.

Each row shows the worker's name, Employee ID, and job title. You can also reach this tab by tapping the Absent stat tile on the Dashboard.

If all workers have clocked in, the tab shows a green confirmation message: "All workers have clocked in today!"

ℹ

The list refreshes each time you open the tab. Absent workers are those with no clock-in record at all for today — a worker who clocked in and then clocked out is considered present, not absent.

Admins Tab (Super Admin Only)

This tab is only visible if you have the super_admin role. It lets you manage admin accounts for your company.

Adding an Admin Account

Click + Add Admin to expand the form.

Enter the admin's Full Name — this is their display name.

Choose a Username — lowercase letters, numbers, and underscores only (e.g. jane_ops).

Set a Password — minimum 6 characters. Share this securely with the admin. They can use the password reset feature later.

Optionally enter an Email address (used for password reset OTP if configured).

Select a Role: Admin (day-to-day) or Super Admin (full company access). As a super_admin you can create both types.

Tap Create Admin. The account will appear in the list immediately.

Managing Existing Admins

Each admin row has action buttons:

  • ✏ Edit — Update name, email, and role.
  • 🔑 Reset Password — Set a new password for the account (direct update, no email required).
  • 🚫 / ✅ — Deactivate or reactivate the admin account.

Setup Tab

The Setup tab shows your company's configuration and gives you the Worker Portal Link to share with staff.

Worker Portal Link

The portal link is in the format:

https://mohlatswa.github.io/WorkerClockIn/?c=YOURCODE

Replace YOURCODE with your company's code as shown in the Setup tab. Share this link with all workers. When a worker opens this link, the app automatically knows which company they belong to — they just need to enter their Employee ID.

📱

You can add this link to a WhatsApp group, email it, print it as a QR code, or add it to a shared phone as a shortcut. Workers can also install it as an app on their phone (see the Worker Guide).

Workplaces

Workplaces define the physical locations where workers are allowed to clock in. Each workplace has a name, GPS coordinates, and a radius (in metres). Workers must be within the radius to activate the Clock In button.

You can configure your workplace directly in the Setup tab — no developer access is required. Fill in the workplace name, address, latitude, longitude, and radius, then tap Save Workplace.

📍

Tip — detect automatically: Tap the 📍 button next to the Address field to fill in the latitude and longitude from your current GPS position. This works best if you are physically standing at the workplace when you do it.

After saving a workplace, edit each worker's record and assign them to it. Workers without a workplace assigned will not be able to clock in — their clock-in button will show "Workplace Not Set Up" until you assign them a location.

ℹ

The default radius is 100 metres. For large sites (farms, warehouses, construction sites) you may need to increase this to 200–500 metres to accommodate the size of the property.

Security — Device Binding

When a worker signs in for the first time, their device is automatically bound to their account. This means their account can only be used from that one device. If they try to sign in from a different phone, they will see a message saying the account is bound to another device.

This prevents workers from sharing accounts or logging in remotely from a location outside the workplace.

When does a worker need their device reset?

  • They got a new phone
  • They cleared their browser's data/storage
  • They are using a shared kiosk device and the original device is no longer available

Security — Account Lockout

After 5 incorrect PIN attempts, a worker's account is locked for 30 minutes. The worker will see a message telling them how long they need to wait. After 30 minutes the lock expires automatically and they can try again.

Similarly, admin accounts lock after 5 incorrect password attempts.

You can unlock a worker's account immediately using the security reset button — without waiting for the 30 minutes to expire.

Resetting Worker Security

The 🏝 Reset Security button (shield icon) in the Workers tab does the following in a single operation:

  • Removes the device binding (so a new device can be registered)
  • Clears any active session token (signs the worker out of any active session)
  • Clears the failed attempts counter
  • Removes any lockout (so the worker can sign in immediately)

A confirmation dialog will appear before the reset is performed. After the reset, the next time the worker signs in from any device, that device becomes the new registered device.

⚠

After a security reset, if the worker was currently clocked in, their session is invalidated but their attendance record is not automatically closed. You may need to manually note the clock-out time in the attendance records.

Auto Midnight Clock-Out

Every night at midnight (SAST), any worker who is still clocked in is automatically clocked out by the system. This prevents attendance records from spanning multiple days and keeps your reports accurate.

Automatic clock-outs are scheduled via a database job that runs at 22:00 UTC (which equals midnight South Africa Standard Time). No admin action is required.

🕒

Workers who are automatically clocked out at midnight will have a clock-out time of exactly 00:00. If a worker legitimately works past midnight, their shift will be split across two days. Use the Force Clock Out option and manually adjust records for payroll if needed.

Troubleshooting

ProblemLikely CauseSolution
Worker says "Employee ID not found" Wrong Employee ID, or worker is deactivated, or opened the wrong company link Check the Employee ID spelling. Check the worker is active (not deactivated). Make sure they used your company's link (?c=YOURCODE).
Worker says "Account is bound to another device" Worker is on a new phone or cleared their browser storage Tap the 🏝 Reset Security button for that worker. They can then sign in from their new device.
Worker is locked out (too many wrong PINs) 5 incorrect PIN attempts Tap 🏝 Reset Security. Alternatively, wait 30 minutes for the lock to expire automatically.
Clock In button stays grey ("Checking Location") GPS is slow to lock, or location permission was denied Ask the worker to allow location access in their browser settings. On iOS, go to Settings → Safari → Location. Wait 30–60 seconds for GPS to lock after allowing permission.
Clock In button shows "Too far from workplace" Worker is outside the geofence radius Verify the worker is at the correct location. If the workplace coordinates are incorrect, update them in the Setup tab — enter the correct latitude/longitude (or tap 📍 while standing at the site) and save. You can also increase the radius if the site is large.
Face recognition keeps saying "Face not recognised" Poor lighting, bad enrolment photo, or threshold too strict Re-enrol the worker in better lighting conditions. Ensure the face is well-lit and fills the oval frame. Glasses or significant appearance changes since enrolment can cause failures.
Biometric button does not appear for a worker Biometric not yet enrolled, or device does not support WebAuthn Check that the worker's device supports fingerprint or Face ID. Enrol using the 📏 button — the worker must be present on the device.
Attendance shows worker clocked in but not out Worker forgot to clock out, or session was interrupted These records remain with status "active". The attendance CSV will show "Still In" for clock-out time. Manually note the actual clock-out time for payroll purposes.
Admin login says "Account disabled" Admin account was deactivated A super_admin or developer must reactivate the account in the Admins tab.
Workers list shows no workers No workers added yet, or you are logged in as the wrong company Verify you are logged into the correct admin account. Add workers using the + Add Worker button.
CSV download is empty or shows "No records found" Date range too narrow, or no attendance records match filters Widen the date range. Try selecting "All Workers" and "All Methods". Check that workers have actually clocked in during the selected period.
Clock In button shows "Workplace Not Set Up" Worker has no workplace assigned Go to the Setup tab and save your workplace location. Then edit the affected worker record and assign them to the workplace. They can clock in immediately after.
Admin forgot password — OTP email not received Email address not saved on the admin account, or email went to spam Check spam/junk. A super admin can update the email address in the Admins tab. The developer can set a new password directly from the Developer panel.
Attendance shows workers clocked out at midnight Auto midnight clock-out (scheduled job) This is normal — any worker still clocked in at midnight is automatically closed off. Workers who genuinely work past midnight will have a split record. Note the actual end time for payroll.

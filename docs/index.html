Quick Start

Get a worker clocked in for the first time

1
Admin logs in and navigates to the Workers tab. Click + Add Worker and fill in Employee ID, Name, Job Title, and PIN.
2
Optionally enrol the worker's face (camera capture) or fingerprint (WebAuthn) for passwordless clock-in.
3
In the Setup tab, enter your workplace name, address, and GPS coordinates (tap 📍 to auto-detect your current location), then tap Save Workplace. Assign the workplace to each worker via their Edit form. Workers cannot clock in without a workplace configured.
4
From the Setup tab, copy the Worker Portal Link (e.g. https://mohlatswa.github.io/WorkerClockIn/?c=COMPANYCODE) and send it to the worker via WhatsApp or email.
5
The worker opens the link, enters their Employee ID, then taps their PIN. On the first sign-in the device is registered automatically.
6
The app checks the worker's GPS distance from the configured workplace. If within range, the Clock In button activates. Workers too far away will see "Too far from workplace".

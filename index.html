Biometric Data Consent

What & why (POPIA notice):
  • What we store: a mathematical template of the face (not a photo) and/or a device fingerprint key — never an actual fingerprint image.
  • Why: only to verify identity at clock-in and prevent buddy-clocking.
  • Who can see it: the employer and Reatlegile Solutions (the system provider).
  • How long: only while the person is an active employee.
  • It is voluntary: a PIN can be used instead. Consent can be withdrawn at any time via the administrator, who will delete the biometric data.
